When the handover of a call from one app to another takes place, there are two devices which are involved in the handover; the initiating and receiving devices. This permission protects access to the TelecomManager.acceptHandover(Uri, int, PhoneAccountHandle) which the receiving side of the handover uses to accept a handover.
